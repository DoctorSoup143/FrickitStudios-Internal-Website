Studio Leadership

Studio Managers

Studio Director

Oversees all aspects of //FrickitStudios and the creative development processes. The Studio Director handles all administrative developments and issues as well as overseeing the Producer and Project Managers.

Producer

The studio Producer oversees all marketing, distribution, licensing, and financial aspects of the studio.

Project Manager

Project Managers are responsible for managing the development team, scheduling and tracking tasks, and ensuring the project is headed toward completion.

Team Leads

Community Manager

Creates marketing materials, interacts with the community, and is the liaison between a game and its audience.

Senior Programmer

The lead programmer. Oversees tasks for, and quality checks, all programming related tasks, Blueprints, and code.

Art Director

Oversees the production of, and quality checks, all art and animation assets.

Senior Level Designer

The lead level designer. Oversees the creation of, and quality checks, all environmental factors including lighting.
